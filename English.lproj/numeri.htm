Welcome to the 'Numeri' puzzle game.

Your goal is to draw a single loop line with no loose ends. The line must circumvent all the numbers in order to make them valid. When the number of line segments around a cell matches the displayed value, the cell is considered valid.

    

Use '>' to show the solution, or crete a new puzzle. Use '<' to clear the puzzle.

Facing the iPhone down reveals the missing numbers in the grid in order to help you solve it.

The full version is a feisty 5x6 grid while the free version uses a simpler 4x4 grid.
